About me

Hi there! My name is Sam Tyrie! I was born on the 3rd of December in Ottawa, Ontario. I love reading mysteries and horror novels, rock climbing, and listening to heavy rock tunes. My favourite band of ALL TIME is Royal Blood. The reason I love Royal Blood so much is not only because of the interesting sounds they create by only being a bassist and drummer, but they also have create such an cool and fun vibe with their music.

Why I joined the IMD Program

After I graduated high school, I knew that I at least wanted to take 1 year off, I didn't know that it would result in two years. I ended up working at a donut shop for those two years as a baker, then after 1 year, the head baker. It was unfulfilling, and ended up getting a personal training certificate to try and get any type of career, but it didn't pan out. I always loved creating, writing, and anything creative, and when I ended up on the Algonquin website and saw the IMD program, I was surprised that it met everything that I was looking to learn, so I joined. I'm currently enjoying my time in the program greatly, and the course delivery from all the professors has been insanely impressive and eye opening. Thank you Prof. Rob for teaching us code!
